ABOUT PROF ADENIJI

Abolade Adeniji is a Research Fellow at the Nigerian Institute of International Affairs (NIIA). He holds a Ph.D degree in History from the University of Lagos, Akoka. His area of specialization is International Economic Relations. He has worked extensively on aspects development studies and the international political economy. He is the editor of the book, Transformations in International Relations since 1945. He has published widely in both local and international He is also the editor (African region) of the American Journal of International Relations and Development.

Here is a timeline of Prof. Adeniji's life
  • 1960-Born in Lagos, Nigeria.
  • 1964-1970 Primary education
  • 1970-1976 Secondary Education
  • 1977-1979 O level
  • 1984- 1988 University Education
  • Academic qualifications include: Wassce, Bsc, pHD.
  • A father to 3 beautiful kids
  • Presently a Professor of History and International relations at Lagos State University, Ojo
